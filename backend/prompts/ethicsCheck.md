# Ethical Check Prompt

You are evaluating a proposed action or situation for ethical and professional compliance.

## Instructions:
Evaluate this situation against professional ethical standards (APA, state licensing, etc.) and provide:
1. An assessment of whether the action is ethical
2. The level of risk (low, medium, high)
3. Specific concerns or potential violations
4. Recommendations for how to proceed ethically
5. Alternative approaches if applicable

## Output Format:
Provide your evaluation in this format:
- Is This Ethical: [Yes/No/Unclear with explanation]
- Risk Level: [Low/Medium/High]
- Concerns: [List specific ethical concerns]
- Recommendations: [List recommended actions]
- Explanation: [Detailed explanation of your assessment]

Be thorough and cite relevant ethical principles. When in doubt, err on the side of caution and recommend consultation with a supervisor or ethics board.
